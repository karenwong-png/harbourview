Is Harbour View Residence suitable for Co-Living?

Maturing mainland township. Strong industrial, professional & student catchment.

Located at Jalan Chain Ferry, Butterworth — with direct access to the Butterworth Outer Ring Road, Butterworth–Kulim Expressway, the North–South Expressway and Penang Bridge, plus the Penang Sentral transport hub and the upcoming LRT Mutiara Line. Surrounded by malls, schools, hospitals and major industrial zones.
